About us

Experience an extraordinary experience in our 5-star accommodation with rooms and suites in the iconic plantation-style "Manor House" or widely spaced in separate luxurious Creole-style "villas" across beautifully landscaped gardens and along the beach. The soft colours and natural materials used in the rooms, combined with the tropical chic design, enhance the harmony of plantation elegance and timeless elegance.

Accommodation

This resort features linen service, blackout curtains and air conditioning in some rooms. Guests will be entertained by cable TV in selected rooms. At this resort accommodation, guests can also enjoy a refrigerator, a coffee or tea maker, instant coffee and instant tea in some rooms. In some bathrooms there is a hair dryer, toiletries and towels, so you will always be clean and fresh.

Spa

Bathrobes and hotel slippers will be provided in your room and a complimentary towel and two sheets will be provided by the wellness reception upon entering the wellness & spa. As part of our environmental stewardship, we will lend you additional towels, sheets or a bathrobe for a fee.
